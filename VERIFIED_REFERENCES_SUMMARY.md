## Source Citation Standards Established

### For Regulatory Requirements
- Must cite: 33 CFR citation or official Coast Guard document
- Must include: Publication date, official URL
- Format: Clear citation with verification status

### For Recommendations
- Must cite: Authoritative source (NIST, CISA, industry standards)
- Must include: Source URL, publication date
- Format: Clear citation with verification status

### For Research Findings
- Must cite: Multiple sources validating finding
- Must include: Source URLs, publication dates
- Format: Clear citation with note if Coast Guard confirmation needed
