# Cybersecurity Training Plan Template

## Purpose

This template provides a structure for developing a Cybersecurity Training Plan that meets MTSA requirements. Customize for your specific facility and training needs.

---

## Training Plan Structure

### 1. Training Program Overview
- Training objectives
- Regulatory requirements
- Target audience
- Training scope

### 2. Training Requirements
- Mandatory training (all IT/OT personnel)
- Role-based training requirements
- Training frequency
- Refresher training requirements

### 3. Training Content
- Core training modules
- Role-specific modules
- Facility-specific content
- Training materials

### 4. Training Delivery
- Delivery methods
- Training schedule
- Training platform
- Instructor requirements

### 5. Training Administration
- Training registration
- Completion tracking
- Recordkeeping
- Reporting

### 6. Training Evaluation
- Effectiveness measurement
- Feedback collection
- Continuous improvement
- Assessment methods

### 7. Appendices
- Training content outlines
- Training materials
- Assessment tools
- Recordkeeping templates

---

## Template Usage

**Customization:**
- Replace placeholders with facility-specific information
- Add facility-specific training content
- Customize for your training delivery method
- Ensure all MTSA requirements are addressed

**Completion:**
- Complete all sections
- Include all required training
- Document training program
- Obtain necessary approvals
