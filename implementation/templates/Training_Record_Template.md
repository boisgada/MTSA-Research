# Cybersecurity Training Record Template

## Purpose

This template provides a structure for recording cybersecurity training completion for MTSA compliance. Use for tracking all required training.

---

## Training Record Fields

### Personnel Information
- Name
- Employee ID
- Department
- Role
- IT/OT Access (Yes/No)

### Training Information
- Training Type
- Training Module/Content
- Training Date
- Completion Date
- Delivery Method
- Instructor/Vendor

### Completion Status
- Status (Completed/In Progress/Not Started)
- Completion Percentage
- Assessment Score (if applicable)
- Certificate Number (if applicable)

### Refresher Training
- Last Refresher Date
- Next Refresher Due Date
- Refresher Status

### Notes
- Additional notes
- Exemptions (if any)
- Special circumstances

---

## Template Usage

**For Each Personnel:**
- Create record for each IT/OT personnel
- Track all training completion
- Maintain for recordkeeping requirements
- Update as training is completed

**Recordkeeping:**
- Maintain complete records
- Retain per MTSA requirements (typically 3-5 years)
- Organize for easy access
- Available for Coast Guard review
