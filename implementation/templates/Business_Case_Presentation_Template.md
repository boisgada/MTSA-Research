# Business Case Presentation Template for MTSA Compliance

## Purpose

This template provides a structure for executive presentations to secure support and funding for MTSA compliance initiatives.

---

## Presentation Structure

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- Project title
- Presenter name
- Date
- Organization

### Slide 2: Executive Summary
- Key points (3-5 bullets)
- Recommendation
- Bottom line (cost, timeline, value)

### Slide 3: Regulatory Requirement
- MTSA mandate overview
- Mandatory deadlines
- Compliance obligation
- Enforcement risk

### Slide 4: Current State
- Current compliance status
- Gaps identified
- Risks of non-compliance
- Current capabilities

### Slide 5: Proposed Solution
- Approach (corporate framework vs. site-by-site)
- Key components
- Implementation phases
- Timeline

### Slide 6: Investment Required
- Total investment
- Breakdown by category
- Resource requirements
- Timeline

### Slide 7: Return on Investment
- Cost savings (if corporate framework)
- Efficiency gains
- Risk reduction
- Compliance assurance
- 5-year ROI

### Slide 8: Risks of Non-Compliance
- Enforcement actions
- Fines and penalties
- Operational restrictions
- Reputational damage
- License to operate

### Slide 9: Implementation Plan
- Phases
- Timeline
- Key milestones
- Resource allocation

### Slide 10: Recommendations
- Recommended approach
- Next steps
- Decision needed
- Questions

---

## Template Usage

**Customization:**
- Replace placeholders with specific information
- Add facility-specific details
- Include data and analysis
- Tailor to audience

**Presentation:**
- Keep concise (10-15 slides)
- Focus on business value
- Use visuals effectively
- Be prepared for questions
