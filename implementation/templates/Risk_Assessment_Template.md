# Cybersecurity Risk Assessment Template

## Purpose

This template provides a structure for conducting risk assessments for critical assets under MTSA. Use for documenting risk identification, analysis, and treatment.

---

## Risk Assessment Structure

### 1. Asset Information
- Asset name
- Asset description
- Asset type (IT/OT)
- Criticality classification
- System dependencies

### 2. Threat Assessment
- Threats identified
- Threat likelihood
- Threat actors
- Threat sources
- Threat trends

### 3. Vulnerability Assessment
- Vulnerabilities identified
- Vulnerability severity
- Exploitability
- Affected systems
- Vulnerability trends

### 4. Impact Assessment
- Operational impact
- TSI potential
- Trade/commerce impact
- Regional economic impact
- Business impact

### 5. Risk Analysis
- Risk identification
- Risk calculation (Likelihood Ã— Impact)
- Risk level (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
- Risk prioritization
- Risk interdependencies

### 6. Risk Treatment
- Treatment strategy (Mitigate/Accept/Transfer/Avoid)
- Treatment actions
- Responsibilities
- Timeline
- Resource requirements

### 7. Risk Monitoring
- Monitoring approach
- Risk indicators
- Review frequency
- Update procedures

### 8. Appendices
- Detailed threat analysis
- Detailed vulnerability analysis
- Supporting documentation
- Risk register

---

## Template Usage

**Assessment:**
- Complete for each critical asset
- Use consistent methodology
- Document thoroughly
- Review regularly

**Risk Treatment:**
- Develop treatment plans
- Prioritize risks
- Implement treatments
- Monitor effectiveness
