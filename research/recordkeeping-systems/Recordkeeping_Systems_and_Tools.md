## 1. Recordkeeping Overview

### 1.1 Recordkeeping Purpose

**Primary Objectives:**
- Demonstrate compliance with MTSA requirements
- Support Coast Guard inspections
- Enable audit readiness
- Maintain compliance history
- Support continuous improvement

### 1.2 Recordkeeping Scope

**Required Records:**
- Cybersecurity Plan and updates
- Critical asset inventory
- Cybersecurity assessments
- Training records
- Drill and exercise records
- Incident reports
- Corrective action plans
- Compliance documentation

### 1.3 Recordkeeping Challenges

**Common Challenges:**
- Volume of records
- Record organization
- Access and retrieval
- Retention management
- Audit trail maintenance

## 5. Retention Policies

### 5.1 Retention Requirements

**MTSA Requirements:**
- Typically 3-5 years minimum
- May vary by record type
- Check specific requirements
- Maintain for audit purposes

### 5.2 Retention Categories

**Retention Categories:**
- Permanent: Plans, assessments
- Long-term: Training, incidents (5+ years)
- Medium-term: Logs, reports (3-5 years)
- Short-term: Temporary records (1-3 years)

### 5.3 Retention Management

**Management:**
- Retention schedules
- Automated retention
- Disposal procedures
- Legal hold procedures
- Archive procedures
