## 1. Drill and Exercise Overview

### 1.1 Regulatory Requirements

**Mandatory Requirements:**
- **2 Drills per year**: Cybersecurity drills
- **1 Exercise per year**: Cybersecurity exercise
- Documentation required
- After-action reviews required

**Drill vs. Exercise:**
- **Drill**: Focused on specific procedures
- **Exercise**: Comprehensive scenario testing

### 1.2 Drill and Exercise Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**
- Test cybersecurity capabilities
- Validate procedures
- Identify improvements
- Train personnel
- Demonstrate compliance
