## 7. Leading vs. Lagging Indicators

### 7.1 Leading Indicators

**Leading Indicators (Predictive):**
- Training completion rates
- Assessment completion
- Documentation status
- Implementation progress
- Gap remediation progress

**Use Cases:**
- Predict compliance status
- Identify risks early
- Enable proactive management
- Support planning

### 7.2 Lagging Indicators

**Lagging Indicators (Outcome):**
- Compliance audit results
- Coast Guard inspection results
- Incident counts
- Enforcement actions
- Non-compliance findings

**Use Cases:**
- Measure outcomes
- Validate effectiveness
- Demonstrate results
- Historical analysis

### 7.3 Balanced Metrics

**Balanced Approach:**
- Combine leading and lagging indicators
- Monitor both predictive and outcome metrics
- Use for comprehensive view
- Support decision-making
