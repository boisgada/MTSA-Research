## 1. CySO Overview

### 1.1 Regulatory Requirement

**Mandatory Requirement:**
- **July 16, 2027**: CySO must be designated
- Must be designated before plan submission
- Must have authority to implement cybersecurity measures

**Designation Requirements:**
- Formal designation
- Documented in Cybersecurity Plan
- Documented in Facility Security Plan
- Communicated to all personnel

### 1.2 CySO Purpose

**Primary Role:**
- Overall responsibility for cybersecurity
- MTSA compliance oversight
- Coast Guard coordination
- Incident response leadership
- Compliance management

## 2. Detailed Responsibilities

### 2.1 Compliance Management

**Compliance Responsibilities:**
- Ensure MTSA compliance
- Oversee compliance activities
- Monitor compliance status
- Report on compliance
- Address compliance issues

### 2.2 Plan Management

**Plan Responsibilities:**
- Develop Cybersecurity Plan
- Maintain and update plan
- Ensure plan implementation
- Coordinate plan updates
- Manage plan documentation

### 2.3 Assessment Management

**Assessment Responsibilities:**
- Oversee annual assessments
- Review assessment results
- Prioritize findings
- Manage corrective actions
- Track remediation

### 2.4 Training Management

**Training Responsibilities:**
- Oversee training program
- Ensure training completion
- Review training effectiveness
- Update training content
- Maintain training records

### 2.5 Incident Response

**Incident Responsibilities:**
- Lead incident response
- Coordinate with teams
- Make reporting decisions
- Coordinate with NRC
- Coordinate with Coast Guard

### 2.6 Coast Guard Coordination

**Coordination Responsibilities:**
- Primary Coast Guard contact
- Coordinate inspections
- Respond to requests
- Manage communications
- Maintain relationships

### 2.7 Technical Oversight

**Technical Responsibilities:**
- Oversee technical measures
- Review security controls
- Assess control effectiveness
- Guide technical decisions
- Ensure implementation
