# Cybersecurity Plan Submission Process Guide

## Purpose

This guide provides step-by-step procedures for submitting the Cybersecurity Plan to the Coast Guard, including required documentation, review timelines, resubmission procedures, and approval criteria.

## 1. Submission Overview

### 1.1 Submission Requirement

**Mandatory Deadline:**
- **July 16, 2027**: Cybersecurity Plan must be submitted to Coast Guard

**Submission Authority:**
- Submit to appropriate Coast Guard District Office
- District Office reviews and approves
- Approval required before implementation (in some cases)

### 1.2 Submission Objectives

**Submission Goals:**
- Meet regulatory deadline
- Obtain Coast Guard approval
- Demonstrate compliance
- Establish compliance baseline
- Enable implementation

## 3. Submission Process

### 3.1 Submission Steps

**Step 1: Finalize Plan**
- Complete all sections
- Verify completeness
- Obtain approvals
- Prepare submission package

**Step 2: Contact District Office**
- Identify appropriate District Office
- Confirm submission procedures
- Clarify requirements
- Schedule submission (if needed)

**Step 3: Submit Plan**
- Submit via required method
- Include all documentation
- Maintain submission confirmation
- Document submission date

**Step 4: Monitor Review**
- Monitor for requests
- Respond promptly
- Provide additional information
- Maintain communication

### 3.2 Submission Methods

**Submission Options:**
- Electronic submission (if available)
- Mail submission
- In-person submission
- Combination of methods

**Method Selection:**
- Check District Office preferences
- Confirm acceptable methods
- Use most appropriate method
- Maintain confirmation

## 5. Coast Guard Review Process

### 5.1 Review Timeline

**Typical Timeline:**
- Initial review: 30-60 days
- Additional information requests: As needed
- Final review: 30-60 days
- Total: 60-120 days (varies by District)

**Timeline Factors:**
- Plan completeness
- District workload
- Complexity of plan
- Quality of submission
- Response timeliness

### 5.2 Review Process

**Review Steps:**
1. Initial receipt and logging
2. Completeness review
3. Technical review
4. Compliance review
5. Additional information requests (if needed)
6. Final review and decision

### 5.3 Review Focus Areas

**Coast Guard Review Focus:**
- Plan completeness
- Requirement coverage
- Technical adequacy
- Integration with FSP
- Implementation feasibility

## 7. Approval Criteria

### 7.1 Approval Types

**Approval Outcomes:**
- **Full Approval**: Plan approved as submitted
- **Conditional Approval**: Approved with requirements
- **Rejection**: Plan rejected, resubmission required

### 7.2 Approval Criteria

**Criteria for Approval:**
- All requirements addressed
- Plan is complete
- Technical adequacy
- Integration with FSP
- Implementation feasibility

### 7.3 Conditional Approval

**Conditional Approval Requirements:**
- Address specific requirements
- Provide additional information
- Implement specified changes
- Meet conditions within timeframe
