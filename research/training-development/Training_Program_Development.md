## 1. Training Requirements Overview

### 1.1 Regulatory Requirements

**Mandatory Deadline:**
- **January 12, 2026**: Initial cybersecurity training for all IT/OT personnel must be completed

**Training Scope:**
- All IT personnel
- All OT personnel
- Personnel with access to critical assets
- Personnel involved in cybersecurity functions

**Training Requirements:**
- MTSA-specific cybersecurity training
- Role-appropriate content
- Regular refresher training
- Training recordkeeping

### 1.2 Training Objectives

**Primary Objectives:**
- Ensure personnel understand MTSA requirements
- Enable personnel to identify and respond to cybersecurity threats
- Support compliance with MTSA regulations
- Maintain security awareness

**Learning Outcomes:**
- Understand MTSA cybersecurity requirements
- Recognize cybersecurity threats
- Know incident reporting procedures
- Understand critical asset protection
- Follow security procedures
