## 1. Industry-Specific Overview

### 1.1 Common MTSA Requirements

**Universal Requirements:**
- All industries must meet the same MTSA cybersecurity requirements (33 CFR Part 101)
- Critical asset identification based on TSI impact (trade/commerce)
- Same deadlines and compliance obligations
- Same documentation and reporting requirements

### 1.2 Industry Variations

**Variations by Industry:**
- Operational characteristics affect critical asset scope
- Industry-specific systems and processes
- Different risk profiles and threat landscapes
- Varying organizational structures
- Different integration with physical security

### 1.3 Implementation Approach

**Approach:**
- Start with universal MTSA requirements
- Apply industry-specific considerations
- Leverage industry best practices
- Adapt templates and frameworks
- Consider industry-specific risks
