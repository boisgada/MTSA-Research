# MTSA Evidence Pack Checklist

Use this list when assembling materials for Coast Guard submission or audits. Tie each item to sources listed in `Cross_Framework_Evidence_Matrix.md`.

---

## Core

- Cybersecurity Plan (with maritime/OT scope) + link to FSP integration
- Scope/SoA including OT assets and segmentation boundaries
- Critical asset register and latest risk assessment (maritime/TSI scenarios)
- NRC/USCG incident reporting playbook with contacts and timestamps

## Technical & Operations

- Network diagrams: IT/DMZ/OT zones, conduits, controls
- Access reviews: privileged/remote/vendor access evidence (IT/OT)
- Patch/vuln status: OT+IT, compensating controls when deferred
- Backup/restore tests: results for critical OT/IT systems
- Logging/monitoring coverage: OT-inclusive, time sync, retention

## Testing & Training

- Drill/exercise schedule and AARs (include OT scenarios)
- Training records: MTSA/OT/NRC content, role-based completion

## Change & Governance

- Change records affecting MTSA scope (config, network, access)
- Versioned history/change log for plan updates
- Evidence matrix cross-reference (appendix)
