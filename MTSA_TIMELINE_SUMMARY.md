# MTSA Cybersecurity Requirements: Complete Timeline Summary

> **Last Updated**: June 2026  
> **Purpose**: Quick reference for all MTSA mandatory deadlines and recommended implementation phases  
> **Source**: Based on U.S. Coast Guard Final Rule "Cybersecurity in the Marine Transportation System" (33 CFR Part 101)

---

## ⚠️ Mandatory Regulatory Deadlines

**These dates are MANDATORY and must be met. Failure to meet these deadlines may result in non-compliance.**

### 🔴 July 16, 2025 - MANDATORY DEADLINE
**Incident Reporting to NRC Becomes Effective**

**Required Actions:**
- ✅ Reporting procedures must be operational
- ✅ Personnel must be trained on incident reporting
- ✅ Detection capabilities must be implemented
- ✅ Must begin reporting cybersecurity incidents to National Response Center (NRC) immediately upon discovery

**Status**: Regulatory requirement - not optional

---

### 🔴 January 12, 2026 - MANDATORY DEADLINE (PASSED)
**Initial Cybersecurity Training Completion**

**Required Actions (at deadline):**
- ✅ Complete initial cybersecurity training for all IT/OT personnel
- ✅ Document training completion
- ✅ Maintain training records

**Current status (after January 12, 2026):**
- Training is now an **ongoing annual** obligation (each person trained within the last 12 months)
- **New personnel**: within **5 days** of system access, no later than **30 days** from hiring
- **New IT/OT systems**: within **5 days** of access
- Coast Guard **Training Verification Job Aid** on [MaritimeCyber](https://www.uscg.mil/MaritimeCyber/) supports inspection readiness

**Status**: Regulatory requirement - not optional

---

### 🔴 July 16, 2027 - MANDATORY DEADLINE
**Three Requirements Due Simultaneously**

**Required Actions:**
1. ✅ **CySO Designation**: Cybersecurity Officer must be designated
2. ✅ **Cybersecurity Assessment**: Initial Cybersecurity Assessment must be completed
3. ✅ **Cybersecurity Plan Submission**: Cybersecurity Plan must be submitted to Coast Guard for approval

**Status**: Regulatory requirements - not optional

---

## 📋 Recommended Implementation Phases

**Note**: The following phases are **recommendations** to ensure you meet the mandatory deadlines above. Starting earlier provides buffer time and ensures readiness.

### Phase 1: Immediate Actions (Now - July 16, 2025)
**Purpose**: Prepare for mandatory incident reporting requirement

**Recommended Actions:**
- Establish incident reporting procedures
- Train personnel on incident reporting
- Implement incident detection capabilities
- Document incident reporting procedures

**Deliverables:**
- Incident reporting procedures
- Trained personnel
- Incident detection capabilities

**⚠️ Mandatory Deadline**: July 16, 2025 - Incident reporting must be operational

---

### Phase 2: Foundation (July 16, 2025 - January 12, 2026)
**Purpose**: Prepare for training deadline and begin work on 2027 deadlines

**Recommended Actions:**
- Designate CySO (recommended early - not mandatory until July 2027, but helpful for implementation)
- Begin Cybersecurity Assessment (recommended to start early - mandatory completion July 2027)
- Develop training program (recommended to complete before January 2026 deadline)
- Begin implementing technical measures (recommended - ongoing requirement)
- Establish recordkeeping system (recommended - ongoing requirement)

**Deliverables:**
- CySO designated (recommended)
- Assessment in progress (recommended)
- Training program developed (recommended)
- Technical measures planning (recommended)
- Recordkeeping system established (recommended)

**⚠️ Mandatory Deadline**: January 12, 2026 - Training must be completed

---

### Phase 3: Training Implementation (Complete by January 12, 2026)
**Purpose**: Meet mandatory training completion requirement

**Mandatory Actions:**
- Complete initial training for all IT/OT personnel (MANDATORY by January 12, 2026)
- Document training completion (MANDATORY)
- Establish annual training schedule (recommended for ongoing compliance)

**Deliverables:**
- All personnel trained (MANDATORY)
- Training records maintained (MANDATORY)
- Annual training schedule (recommended)

**⚠️ Mandatory Deadline**: January 12, 2026 - All IT/OT personnel must complete training

---

### Phase 4: Assessment and Planning (January 2026 - July 2027)
**Purpose**: Complete work required for mandatory July 2027 deadlines

**Actions:**
- Complete Cybersecurity Assessment (MANDATORY completion by July 16, 2027)
- Develop Cybersecurity Plan (MANDATORY submission by July 16, 2027)
- Implement technical measures (recommended - ongoing requirement)
- Conduct initial drills (recommended - 2 per year required)
- Integrate with FSP (recommended - required for plan approval)

**Deliverables:**
- Completed assessment (MANDATORY by July 16, 2027)
- Developed Cybersecurity Plan (MANDATORY submission by July 16, 2027)
- Implemented technical measures (recommended)
- Conducted drills (recommended - ongoing requirement)
- Integrated with FSP (recommended)

**⚠️ Mandatory Deadlines**: July 16, 2027 - Assessment completion, CySO designation, and Plan submission required

---

### Phase 5: Submission and Approval (Complete by July 16, 2027)
**Purpose**: Meet mandatory submission deadline

**Mandatory Actions:**
- Finalize Cybersecurity Plan (MANDATORY - must be ready for submission)
- Submit to Coast Guard for approval (MANDATORY by July 16, 2027)
- Address any feedback (recommended - may be required for approval)
- Obtain approval (recommended - required for compliance)

**Deliverables:**
- Finalized Cybersecurity Plan (MANDATORY)
- Submitted to Coast Guard (MANDATORY by July 16, 2027)
- Approved plan (recommended - required for compliance)

**⚠️ Mandatory Deadline**: July 16, 2027 - Cybersecurity Plan must be submitted to Coast Guard

---

### Phase 6: Ongoing Compliance (Continuous)
**Purpose**: Maintain ongoing compliance with MTSA requirements

**Ongoing Requirements:**
- Annual assessments (mandatory - annually after July 2027)
- Annual training (mandatory - annually after January 2026)
- Drills/exercises (mandatory - 2 drills + 1 exercise per year)
- Recordkeeping (mandatory - ongoing)
- Incident reporting (mandatory - immediate upon discovery)

**Deliverables:**
- Annual compliance
- Updated plans
- Trained personnel
- Tested procedures
- Maintained records

## Key Takeaways

### Critical Deadlines
1. **July 16, 2025**: Incident reporting to NRC becomes mandatory
2. **January 12, 2026**: Initial cybersecurity training must be completed
3. **July 16, 2027**: CySO designation, Assessment completion, and Plan submission required

### Recommended Approach
- **Start Early**: Begin implementation immediately to ensure readiness
- **Phased Implementation**: Use recommended phases to meet deadlines
- **Buffer Time**: Starting earlier provides buffer time for unexpected delays
- **Continuous Compliance**: Plan for ongoing requirements after initial deadlines

### Ongoing Requirements
After initial deadlines, facilities must maintain:
- Annual assessments (annually after July 2027)
- Annual training (annually after January 2026)
- Drills/exercises (2 drills + 1 exercise per year)
- Recordkeeping (ongoing)
- Incident reporting (immediate upon discovery)
