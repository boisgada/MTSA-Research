# Synthesis Folder

## Purpose

This folder contains synthesized research findings, insights, and compiled knowledge derived from research activities and analysis.

---

## Contents

This folder typically contains:
- **Key insights**: Synthesized insights from research
- **Research summaries**: Comprehensive research summaries
- **Analysis documents**: Compiled analysis and findings
- **Talking points**: Prepared talking points or key messages
- **Patterns and themes**: Identified patterns across research

---

## When to Use This Folder

Use the synthesis folder for:
- Compiling findings from multiple research sessions
- Creating summary documents from detailed research
- Preparing materials for presentations or discussions
- Identifying patterns and themes across research
- Creating reusable knowledge artifacts

---

## Organization

You may organize synthesis documents by:
- **Topic**: By research topic or theme
- **Date**: Chronological organization
- **Type**: By document type (insights, summaries, analysis)
- **Audience**: If synthesized for different audiences

---

## Relationship to Other Folders

- **Research folder**: Detailed research notes and sources
- **Deliverables folder**: Final outputs prepared from synthesis
- **Documentation folder**: Template documentation and guides

---

## Best Practices

1. **Cite Sources**: Reference original research when synthesizing
2. **Date Documents**: Include dates on synthesis documents
3. **Version Control**: Track evolution of synthesized knowledge
4. **Clear Structure**: Organize synthesis documents for easy navigation
5. **Regular Updates**: Update synthesis as new research is completed
